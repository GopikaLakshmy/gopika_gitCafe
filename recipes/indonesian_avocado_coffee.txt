Indonesian Avocado Coffee, also known as "Kopi Alpukat," is a unique and refreshing drink combining the richness of coffee and the creaminess of avocado. Here's a recipe:
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados
1 shot of strong brewed coffee (Indonesian coffee beans like Arabica or Robusta)
1 tablespoon sweetened condensed milk
1 tablespoon sugar (or to taste)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Ice cubes
Whipped cream (optional)
Instructions:
Peel and pit the avocados.
Blend avocado flesh, coffee, sweetened condensed milk, sugar, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth.
Taste and adjust sweetness.
Pour into glasses.
Add ice cubes.
Top with whipped cream (if using).
Serve immediately.
Variations:
Add a pinch of salt to balance sweetness.
Use coconut milk or almond milk for a non-dairy version.
Mix in cocoa powder for a mocha flavor.
Top with chocolate shavings or chopped nuts.
Traditional Indonesian Coffee:
Use Kopi Tubruk (Indonesian traditional coffee) for a stronger flavor.
Add a splash of coconut milk or palm sugar syrup.

Tips:
Choose ripe avocados for the best flavor.
Adjust sweetness to taste.
Experiment with different coffee beans and roasts.
Enjoy your delicious Indonesian Avocado Coffee!
