Here's a delicious recipe for Maple Oat Sticky Cake:
Ingredients:
For the cake:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup rolled oats
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup pure maple syrup
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
For the sticky maple sauce:
1/2 cup pure maple syrup
1/4 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
**Instructions:]
Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract.
Gradually mix in flour mixture until just combined.
Pour batter into prepared baking dish.
Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
For the sticky maple sauce:
In a small saucepan, combine maple syrup, heavy cream, butter, and vanilla extract.
Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce heat to low.
Simmer for 5-7 minutes or until sauce thickens.
**Assembly:]
Remove cake from oven and let cool for 10 minutes.
Poke holes all over cake with a skewer.
Pour sticky maple sauce evenly over cake.
Let cake absorb sauce before serving.
**Tips and Variations:]
Use high-quality pure maple syrup for the best flavor.
Substitute chopped nuts or seeds for added texture.
Serve with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
