Italian Cream Iced Coffee, also known as "Caffè Shakerato" or "Caffè Freddo," is a refreshing summer drink that combines espresso, milk, and sugar. Here's a recipe:
Ingredients:
2 shots of strong espresso (Italian roast)
1 cup milk (whole, 2%, or skim)
1 tablespoon granulated sugar (or to taste)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Ice cubes
Whipped cream (optional)
Instructions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
Add espresso, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds.
Strain into a glass filled with ice cubes.
Top with whipped cream (if using).
Variations:
Use coffee instead of espresso for a milder flavor.
Add a splash of liqueur (Kahlúa, Baileys, or Amaretto).
Mix in chocolate syrup or cocoa powder.
Top with cinnamon, cocoa powder, or chocolate shavings.
Italian-Style Iced Coffee:
Use a Moka pot or stovetop espresso maker.
Add sugar to espresso while still hot.
Mix with cold milk and ice.

Tips:
Adjust sugar to taste.
Use high-quality espresso for the best flavor.
Experiment with different milks (almond, soy, or coconut).
Enjoy your delicious Italian Cream Iced Coffee!
