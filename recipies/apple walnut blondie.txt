Here's a delicious recipe for Apple Walnut Blondie:
Ingredients:
For the blondie:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup diced apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
For the streusel topping (optional):
1/4 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract.
Gradually mix in flour mixture until just combined.
Stir in diced apples and chopped walnuts.
Pour batter into prepared baking dish.
If using streusel topping, mix ingredients in a small bowl until crumbly. Sprinkle over batter.
Bake for 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Let cool completely before cutting into squares.
Tips and Variations:
Use a mix of sweet and tart apples for the best flavor.
Substitute pecans or hazelnuts for walnuts.
Add a sprinkle of cinnamon or nutmeg for extra spice.
Make individual blondies in a muffin tin for a snack or dessert.
Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
